### 1. SparseX: Efficient Segment-Level KV Cache Sharing for Interleaved LLM Serving

**🔗 arXiv:** [2606.01751](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.01751) | **🏷️** cs.PF  
**👤** Quqing Zhang, Kai Chen, Ning Liao, Zehao Lin, Bo Tang

**摘要 (EN):**  
In long-context LLM serving, the prefill stage often dominates time-to-first-token and computational cost. Although Prefix Cache in vLLM/PagedAttention has been widely used to reuse identical prompt prefixes, repeated content in practical applications frequently appears as non-prefix, cross-request, cross-turn, and cross-agent segments, which makes conventional cache mechanisms insufficient. This paper presents SparseX, a segment-level KV Cache sharing method for common serving scenarios. SparseX uses contiguous token segments as reuse units and exploits Sparse-Q indices that naturally arise in KV Cache reuse workloads to estimate the key tokens that require correction. Based on this estimate, SparseX performs Sparse-KV Recomputation within a single forward pass, thereby restoring cross-segment contextual interactions under complex interleaved reuse patterns while avoiding additional models or separate preprocessing stages for token selection. SparseX further implements a full+sparse hybrid attention mode based on a layer-specific threshold: early layers retain full attention to obtain a more stable token-importance signal, and later layers switch to sparse recomputation to improve reuse quality on complex long-context tasks. We implement SparseX-vLLM on top of vLLM, integrating segment-level cache lookup, PagedAttention management, RoPE alignment, Sparse-Q token selection, and FlashAttention backends into a unified execution path. SparseX is model-agnostic, training-free, and compatible with Prefix Cache, and it provides unified support for common online serving scenarios including multi-round chat, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and agent workflows.

**📖 中文翻译：**  
在长上下文 LLM 服务中，prefill 阶段通常是首 token 延迟和计算开销的主要瓶颈。虽然 vLLM/PagedAttention 中的 Prefix Cache 已被广泛用于复用相同的前缀，但实际应用中重复内容经常以非前缀片段形式出现（跨请求、跨轮次、跨 agent），传统缓存机制无法应对。本文提出 SparseX，一种面向常见服务场景的**段级 KV Cache 共享方法**。SparseX 以连续 token 段为复用单元，利用 KV Cache 复用工作负载中自然产生的 Sparse-Q 索引来估计需要修正的关键 token，在单次前向传播中执行 Sparse-KV 重计算，恢复复杂交织复用模式下的跨段上下文交互。SparseX 还实现了基于层阈值的全量+稀疏混合注意力模式。基于 vLLM 实现的 SparseX-vLLM 统一了段级缓存查找、PagedAttention、RoPE 对齐、Sparse-Q token 选择及 FlashAttention 后端，支持多轮对话、RAG、Agent 工作流等场景。

### 2. Observation, Not Prediction: Conversation-Level Disaggregated Scheduling for Agentic Serving

**🔗 arXiv:** [2606.01839](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.01839) | **🏷️** cs.DC, cs.AR, cs.LG  
**👤** Jianru Ding, Ryien Hosseini, Pouya Mahdi Gholami, Mingyuan Xiang, Henry Hoffmann

**摘要 (EN):**  
LLM-based agents resolve a user task through many turns of dependent inference and tool calls, producing a workload whose total cost is unknown when the task arrives. Existing multi-turn systems keep the turn as the scheduling unit and decide, turn by turn, whether to disaggregate prefill from decode. That decision rests on the turn's decode length, tool behavior, and KV growth, quantities that are not observable when the scheduler must act, forcing the system to predict them. We show this dependence on prediction is imposed by the scheduling unit, not the workload. Raising the scheduling unit from the turn to the conversation converts turn-level irregularity into a stable, two-phase structure: 1) a compute-bound turn-1 prefill followed by 2) a long, memory-bound tail. Thus, with the conversation as the scheduling unit, placement reduces to reading the first-turn input length and per-decoder KV occupancy, both directly observable. We instantiate this principle in ConServe, which routes the first-turn prefill to a high-throughput prefiller, transfers the KV cache exactly once, and pins the conversation to a single decoder for its entire tail, with no learned model of decode-side cost. Against a per-turn prediction baseline, ConServe reduces p95 time-to-first-effective-token (the latency of a conversation's first user-visible output) by 51.08% and improves energy efficiency by 7.51% while preserving last-turn TBT and SLOs; mapping the two phases onto heterogeneous GPU tiers adds a further 22.75% in energy efficiency.

**📖 中文翻译：**  
基于 LLM 的 Agent 通过多轮推理和工具调用来完成用户任务，工作负载总量在任务到达时未知。现有系统以"轮次"为调度单元，逐轮决定是否分离 prefill 和 decode，但该决策依赖不可观测的 decode 长度、工具行为和 KV 增长量，迫使系统进行预测。本文证明这种对预测的依赖源于调度单元而非工作负载本身。将调度单元从"轮次"提升到"对话"级别后，轮次级的不规则性转化为稳定的两阶段结构：计算密集的第 1 轮 prefill + 长尾内存受限阶段。基于此原理实现的 **ConServe** 将首轮 prefill 路由到高吞吐 prefiller，KV cache 只传输一次，并将整个对话的 decode 阶段固定到单个 decoder。相比逐轮预测基线，ConServe 将 p95 首有效 token 延迟降低 51.08%，能效提升 7.51%。

### 3. Scaling LLM Inference Beyond Amdahl's Limits via Eliminating Non-Scalable Overheads

**🔗 arXiv:** [2606.01927](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.01927) | **🏷️** cs.DC  
**👤** Alan Zhao, Cyril Y. He, Wei Xu

**摘要 (EN):**  
Deployers of online LLM services usually seek to maximize cluster-wide performance given a fixed number of GPUs. Tensor parallelism (TP) is necessary to fit modern models but scales sub-linearly as the TP degree t grows, due to cross-GPU communication and non-scalable runtime work, as predicted by Amdahl's Law. Conversely, increasing t improves memory efficiency and alleviates KV-cache contention and swapping. We identify and validate an empirical optimal TP degree t_e that balances these effects. We present Albireo, a parallel inference system that raises the attainable t_e by shrinking the non-scalable portion via overlap of scheduling and I/O with compute and sequence-parallel sampling, without changing model architectures. Across models and benchmarks, Albireo achieves up to 1.9x higher throughput, 48% lower latency, 28% higher GPU utilization, and 54% lower energy than vLLM; in production it yields up to 2x higher throughput.

**📖 中文翻译：**  
在线 LLM 服务通常希望用固定数量的 GPU 最大化集群吞吐。张量并行（TP）虽然是大模型部署所必需的，但随着 TP 度数增长，跨 GPU 通信和不可扩展的运行时开销导致缩放呈次线性（Amdahl 定律）。另一方面，增大 TP 度数可改善内存效率并缓解 KV cache 争用和交换。本文识别并验证了经验最优 TP 度数 t_e，并提出 **Albireo**——一个通过将调度和 I/O 与计算及序列并行采样重叠来缩小不可扩展部分、从而提升可达 t_e 的并行推理系统。相比 vLLM，Albireo 吞吐最高提升 1.9 倍，延迟降低 48%，GPU 利用率提升 28%，能耗降低 54%。

### 4. Extreme Low-Bit Inference in Reasoning Models: Failure Modes and Targeted Recovery

**🔗 arXiv:** [2606.02011](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02011) | **🏷️** cs.AI, cs.LG  
**👤** Ekaterina Alimaskina, Darya Rudas, Denis Shveykin, Gleb Molodtsov, Pavel Vasiliev

**摘要 (EN):**  
Large Reasoning Models (LRMs) rely on long reasoning traces, making inference expensive. While low-bit quantization reduces per-token decoding cost, we show that aggressive 2-bit inference can fail to deliver end-to-end speedup because instability in the generation process inflates total token count. Instead of merely lowering answer accuracy, 2-bit quantization often produces much longer traces with repetitive loops, budget exhaustion, delayed commitment, and unclosed reasoning segments. We analyze full reasoning traces of Qwen3 reasoning models across mathematical and commonsense benchmarks and show that accuracy degradation is tightly linked to these process-level failures. To address them, we introduce two lightweight controls: FP16 planning, which gives the 2-bit model a short high-precision outline, and loop rescue, which detects repetitive traces and either commits to an earlier answer or falls back to FP16. On MATH-500, loop rescue improves Qwen3-8B accuracy from 17.2% to 74.2%, while planning plus loop rescue improves Qwen3-32B from 65.0% to 87.2%. Our results show that extreme low-bit reasoning becomes practical when its failures are treated as controllable generation pathologies.

**📖 中文翻译：**  
大型推理模型（LRM）依赖长推理链，导致推理成本高昂。虽然低比特量化能降低每 token 的解码成本，但本文发现激进的 2 比特推理**无法带来端到端加速**——因为生成过程的不稳定性会膨胀总 token 数。2 比特量化不仅降低答案准确性，还常产生更长的推理轨迹（重复循环、预算耗尽、延迟承诺、未闭合推理段）。本文对 Qwen3 推理模型的完整推理轨迹进行了分析，提出 FP16 规划（提供短的高精度大纲）和循环救援（检测重复轨迹并回退）两种轻量级控制方法。在 MATH-500 上，循环救援将 Qwen3-8B 准确率从 17.2% 提升至 74.2%。

### 5. Forget Attention: Importance-Aware Attention Is All You Need (SISA)

**🔗 arXiv:** [2606.02332](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02332) | **🏷️** cs.AI, cs.CL, cs.LG  
**👤** Soohyeong Shin, Yeongwook Yang

**摘要 (EN):**  
Combining attention's global retrieval with the sequential importance signal of state space models (SSMs) is the open challenge of hybrid language modeling. Transformers see everywhere but cannot prioritize; SSMs know what matters but cannot revisit. Existing hybrids — Jamba (block level) and Hymba (head level) — place the two in separate compartments, so neither informs the other during the attention computation itself. We propose SISA (SSM-Informed Softmax Attention), which adds an SSM-derived importance term directly inside the attention score and realizes the full operation as a single SDPA call on augmented query/key vectors — no recurrent state, no custom kernel. At 152M / 5B tokens, SISA reaches LAMBADA-greedy 17.3% (vs. Transformer 13.9 and Mamba-3 15.5) and attains NIAH 100% from step 1K, 7x faster than Transformer's retrieval convergence. SISA defines a third design axis for SSM-attention hybrids — score-level fusion — beyond the block-level and head-level paradigms.

**📖 中文翻译：**  
将注意力的全局检索能力与状态空间模型（SSM）的顺序重要性信号相结合，是混合语言建模的开放挑战。Transformer 能"看到"所有位置但无法区分优先级；SSM 知道什么重要但无法回溯。现有混合方案（Jamba 的块级、Hymba 的头级）将两者放在独立模块中，使得注意力计算过程中两者互不感知。本文提出 **SISA（SSM-Informed Softmax Attention）**，将 SSM 派生的重要性项直接加入注意力分数中，通过增强的 query/key 向量以单次 SDPA 调用实现全部操作——无需循环状态，无需定制 kernel。在 152M/5B token 训练下，SISA 的 LAMBADA-greedy 达 17.3%（Transformer 13.9，Mamba-3 15.5），NIAH 检索从第 1K 步起即达 100%，比 Transformer 快 7 倍。SISA 定义了 SSM-Attention 混合的第三条设计轴——**分数级融合**。

### 6. Massive Spikes in LLMs are Bias Vectors: Mechanistic Uncovering and Spike-Free Quantization (INSERTQUANT)

**🔗 arXiv:** [2606.02288](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02288) | **🏷️** cs.LG  
**👤** Yung-Chin Chen, Chung Peng Lee, Ze-Wei Liou, Naveen Verma

**摘要 (EN):**  
Massive activation spikes in Large Language Models (LLMs) severely degrade quantization by stretching dynamic ranges. While prior hypotheses characterize these as high-level scalar biases, we argue that they are merely the scalar intermediates of rigid, structural vector biases in the spike-carrying tokens. We show that these tokens converge to constant vectors after normalization that drive the attention sink and value-state drain mechanisms. We geometrically substantiate this by analyzing the coordination of projection weights: W_K contrastively amplifies the vector, W_Q aligns semantic tokens toward it, and W_V projects it into the spectral null-space. Furthermore, we reveal that the model actively preserves these structural biases against Rotary Positional Embedding (RoPE) perturbations by localizing them in "zones of rotational stability" utilizing low-frequency bands and coherent channel pairs. Leveraging this, we propose INSERTQUANT, a post-training quantization (PTQ) framework that clamps spikes and restores their function via pre-computed template vectors. This renders activations strictly spike-free, enabling robust low-bit quantization with high fidelity. INSERTQUANT achieves parity with state-of-the-art per-tensor quantization methods on LLMs and uniquely generalizes beyond text to other modalities such as ViTs.

**📖 中文翻译：**  
大语言模型中的**大规模激活尖峰**（activation spikes）通过拉伸动态范围严重损害量化效果。本文论证这些尖峰并非独立的高层标量偏置，而是 spike 承载 token 中刚性结构化向量偏置的标量中间产物。研究表明，这些 token 在归一化后会收敛到常向量，驱动 attention sink 和 value-state drain 机制。通过分析投影权重的协调性，论文从几何角度进行了论证。进一步，模型通过将结构偏置定位在"旋转稳定区"（利用低频带和相干通道对）来对抗 RoPE 扰动。基于此发现，本文提出 **INSERTQUANT**——一种训练后量化框架，通过钳制尖峰并用预计算模板向量恢复其功能，实现严格无尖峰激活，支持高保真低比特量化。INSERTQUANT 在 LLM 上达到了与最先进的逐张量量化方法相当的水平，并能泛化到 ViT 等其他模态。

### 7. On the Scaling of PEFT: Towards Million Personal Models of Trillion Parameters (MinT)

**🔗 arXiv:** [2606.02437](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02437) | **🏷️** cs.LG, cs.CL  
**👤** Mind Lab, Song Cao, Vic Cao, Kaijie Chen 等

**摘要 (EN):**  
Parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT) is usually treated as a cheaper alternative to full fine-tuning. We study a broader role: small trainable adapters as persistent local state on top of strong shared foundation models. In this framing, the base model provides shared competence while adapters carry instance-specific behavior such as preferences, skills, tool habits, and memory-like updates. We organize the problem around three scaling axes: Scale Up, where stronger shared priors make small local updates more useful; Scale Down, where we study how small adapters can be while remaining reliable; and Scale Out, where many persistent adapted instances coexist. MinT provides one infrastructure example for managing adapter identity, revision, provenance, evaluation, and serving residency. Together, the results suggest that PEFT can be a compact substrate for persistent personal models rather than only a budget substitute for full fine-tuning.

**📖 中文翻译：**  
参数高效微调（PEFT）通常被视为全参数微调的廉价替代品。本文研究了 PEFT 更广泛的角色：将小型可训练适配器作为共享基础模型上的持久化本地状态。基础模型提供共享能力，适配器承载实例特有行为（偏好、技能、工具习惯、类记忆更新）。论文从三个缩放维度组织问题：Scale Up（更强的共享先验使小型局部更新更有用）、Scale Down（研究适配器能多小仍保持可靠）和 Scale Out（多个持久化实例共存）。**MinT** 提供了一个管理适配器身份、版本、来源、评估和服务驻留的基础设施示例。结果表明，PEFT 可以作为持久个性化模型的紧凑基板。

### 8. Not All Errors Are Equal: A Systematic Study of Error Propagation in Large Language Model Inference

**🔗 arXiv:** [2606.02430](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02430) | **🏷️** cs.DC, cs.AI  
**👤** Yafan Huang, Sheng Di, Guanpeng Li

**摘要 (EN):**  
Large language models (LLMs) are increasingly integrated into high-performance computing (HPC) workflows, accelerating scientific discovery through diverse perspectives such as code generation and domain-specific decision-making. Yet, how soft errors propagate and affect LLM inference remains largely unexplored. To bridge this gap, we present a comprehensive study on error propagation in LLM inference, enabled by our proposed LLMFI, a configurable and deterministic fault-injection framework. Using LLMFI, we systematically inject faults across three open-weighted LLMs and thirteen representative tasks, covering reasoning, multilingual, mathematical, and coding domains. In addition, we conduct fine-grained case studies that reveal critical vulnerability patterns. Overall, our study yields 17 takeaways that advance the understanding of error propagation in LLM inference and introduces four low-overhead directions to improve reliability through software-only modification, offering practical guidance for future error detection and mitigation.

**📖 中文翻译：**  
大语言模型越来越多地被集成到 HPC 工作流中，但软错误如何在 LLM 推理中传播并影响结果仍不明确。本文通过 **LLMFI**（一个可配置的确定性故障注入框架）对 LLM 推理中的错误传播进行了全面研究。在三个开源权重 LLM 和 13 个代表性任务上系统注入故障，涵盖推理、多语言、数学和编程领域。研究得出 17 项重要发现，揭示了关键脆弱性模式，并提出了四条通过纯软件修改提高可靠性的低开销方向。

### 9. CHIMERA: A Flexible and Scalable 3.1 TOPS/W AI-MCU with Transformer Accelerator

**🔗 arXiv:** [2606.02358](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02358) | **🏷️** cs.AR  
**👤** Lorenzo Leone, Philip Wiese, Gamze İslamoğlu, Michael Rogenmoser, Davide Rossi

**摘要 (EN):**  
We present Chimera, a flexible and scalable Microcontroller Unit (MCU) designed to accelerate real-time inference of rapidly evolving transformer-based models at the ultra-low-power edge (hundred of mW). The chip, implemented in 22 nm FDX technology, integrates a transformer accelerator tightly coupled within a compute cluster featuring nine general-purpose RV32IMA cores. Scalability extends to the memory hierarchy through a novel L2 memory island subsystem, which enables data sharing across multiple clusters while delivering 563 Gb/s aggregate bandwidth. The L2 subsystem enforces quality-of-service guarantees for latency-critical traffic, achieving up to 16x latency reduction. Chimera achieves peak energy and area efficiencies of 3.1 TOPS/W and 281 GOPS/mm², demonstrating 1.37x higher energy efficiency and up to 100x higher area efficiency compared to State of the Art (SoA) SoCs. Compared to SoA standalone accelerators, Chimera achieves comparable energy efficiency and up to 1.8x higher area efficiency.

**📖 中文翻译：**  
**Chimera** 是一款灵活可扩展的微控制器单元（MCU），专为在超低功耗边缘（百毫瓦级）加速 Transformer 模型的实时推理而设计。芯片采用 22 nm FDX 工艺实现，集成了紧耦合在计算集群内的 Transformer 加速器及九个通用 RV32IMA 核心。通过创新的 L2 内存岛子系统实现跨集群数据共享，提供 563 Gb/s 聚合带宽，并为延迟敏感流量提供 QoS 保障（延迟降低最高 16 倍）。Chimera 的峰值能效和面积效率分别为 3.1 TOPS/W 和 281 GOPS/mm²。

### 10. O-POPE: High-Frequency Pipelined Outer Product based GEMM Acceleration

**🔗 arXiv:** [2606.02333](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02333) | **🏷️** cs.AR  
**👤** Danilo Cammarata, Angelo Garofalo, Luca Benini

**摘要 (EN):**  
General matrix multiply (GEMM) dominates both execution time and energy consumption of modern machine learning (ML) workloads, placing increasing pressure on hardware efficiency. While quantization mitigates computational and data movement costs, accuracy-sensitive tasks such as training still require higher-precision floating-point formats. Existing floating-point GEMM accelerators face trade-offs between operating frequency, arithmetic utilization, and buffering overhead. This work presents O-POPE, a scalable outer-product engine that achieves concurrently high utilization, low overhead, and a fast operating frequency by repurposing floating-point unit (FPU) pipeline registers as buffers. This solution leverages the data-reuse advantages of output-stationary outer-product execution and enables 1 GHz (0.72 V) operation in 12 nm FINFET technology with less than 2% buffer area for a 2048-MACs configuration. O-POPE achieves up to 99.97% FPU utilization and improves performance (1.33x), performance density by 9%, and energy efficiency by 8%.

**📖 中文翻译：**  
通用矩阵乘法（GEMM）是当前 ML 工作负载中执行时间和能耗的主要部分。现有浮点 GEMM 加速器在运行频率、算术利用率和缓冲开销之间存在权衡。**O-POPE** 是一种可扩展的外积引擎，通过将浮点单元（FPU）流水线寄存器重新用作缓冲区，同时实现了高利用率、低开销和快速运行频率。该方案利用输出固定的外积执行的数据复用优势，在 12 nm FINFET 工艺下实现 1 GHz（0.72 V）运行，2048-MACs 配置的缓冲面积不到 2%。O-POPE 达到 99.97% 的 FPU 利用率，性能提升 1.33 倍，性能密度提升 9%，能效提升 8%。

### 11. Night-Window Batching versus Carbon-Aware Scheduling for Clinical AI GPU Workloads

**🔗 arXiv:** [2606.01766](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.01766) | **🏷️** cs.DC, cs.ET  
**👤** Nishi Doshi, Shrey Shah

**摘要 (EN):**  
Hospitals run more machine learning on GPUs while the carbon footprint of grid electricity rises and falls through the day. Using a computer simulation, we compare 13 scheduling rules on mixed GPU hardware, with synthetic patient-style jobs, urgency tiers, and time-of-day carbon traces. We ask whether running non-urgent jobs overnight is almost as good as a richer rule that mixes urgency and carbon (CUCA). On an eight-GPU baseline, the overnight rule closes about 78% of the carbon gap between urgency-only and CUCA while missing fewer urgent deadlines than either. At 48 jobs per hour, the carbon footprints almost tie, yet the overnight rule still misses fewer urgent deadlines. Overnight batching stays competitive on average modelled carbon; carbon-only rules belong only in stress tests.

**📖 中文翻译：**  
医院在 GPU 上运行越来越多的机器学习任务，而电网的碳足迹在一天中不断变化。本文通过计算机仿真比较了 13 种调度规则在混合 GPU 硬件上的表现。研究发现，**夜间批处理**（将非紧急作业安排在夜间运行）是一种简单高效的碳感知 GPU 调度策略——约能弥合纯紧急度调度与 CUCA（混合紧急度和碳感知调度）之间 78% 的碳排放差距，且紧急任务错过截止时间的比例更低。在每小时 48 个作业的高负载下，碳足迹几乎持平，而夜间规则仍能保持更低的紧急任务超时率。
