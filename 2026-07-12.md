### SMetric: Rethink LLM Scheduling for Serving Agents with Balanced Session-centric Scheduling

- **ArXiv:** [`2607.08565`](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08565v1) | **PDF:** [📄](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.08565v1)
- **作者:** Jiahao Wang, Kaizhan Lin, Kaixi Zhang, Jinbo Han, Xingda Wei, Sijie Shen et al.
- **标签:** `cs.DC, cs.AI`

**中文摘要：**

本文重新审视了面向 Agent 场景的 LLM 调度问题。Agent 场景与传统对话场景不同：Agent 只有在收到完整响应后才行动，使得集群的每秒令牌数（TPS）成为主要目标；且请求间 KV 缓存重用率超过 80%（对话仅 54-62%）。现有调度器为追求缓存重用过度集中路由请求，导致部分实例超载而其他闲置。本文提出 SMetric，一种均衡的会话中心调度策略：每个会话的首个请求按负载均衡路由，后续请求按缓存感知路由。实验表明，在预填充-解码同部署场景下 TPS 提升 10-16%，在分离架构下预填充 TPS 提升 2-34%。

**English Abstract:**

LLM scheduling is critical to serving, yet it remains unclear how well existing designs fit agentic serving—with LLM requests issued by agents instead of humans. This shifts the workload in two ways: (1) agents act only on complete responses, making the cluster's tokens per second (TPS) the primary goal and relaxing per-token latency requirements; and (2) requests share much of their KV cache—reuse exceeds 80% of request tokens in a production trace, versus 54-62% in chat. This paper presents SMetric with balanced session-centric scheduling: it routes each session's first request purely for load balance and its follow-up requests in a cache-aware manner. SMetric improves cluster TPS by 10-16% under prefill-decode colocation and prefill TPS by 2-34% under disaggregation over state-of-the-art schedulers.

### Think Before You Grid-Search: Floor-First Triage for LLM Serving

- **ArXiv:** [`2607.05876`](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05876v2) | **PDF:** [📄](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.05876v2)
- **作者:** Yihua Liu
- **标签:** `cs.PF, cs.AI, cs.DC`

**中文摘要：**

本文提出了一种分析驱动的 LLM 服务优化方法论——"Floor First"（先找下限）。每个解码步骤被建模为一个五维资源向量（HBM 字节、FLOPs、网络字节、网络消息数、KV 容量），通过资源求和与跨资源取最大值得到性能下限区间。测量结果在此区间内的位置可读出重叠质量，无需打开性能分析器。以 DeepSeek-V3.2 风格 671B MoE/MLA 模型在 16×H20 GPU 上的案例研究显示：TP16 解码约在 70 个并发 8K 请求时受 KV 容量限制；稀疏注意力移除了 KV 带宽项但无法突破容量墙；EP16+DP-attention 布局将容量墙提升至约 644 但以单流延迟为代价。

**English Abstract:**

This paper argues for a residual-driven triage workflow—Floor First—for LLM serving optimization. Each decode step is modeled as a five-dimensional resource vector (HBM bytes, FLOPs, network bytes, network messages, KV capacity); summing within a resource and maximizing across resources gives an optimistic floor, the plain sum a pessimistic one. Where a measurement lands inside this [max, sum] interval reads out overlap quality before any profiler is opened. A case study of DeepSeek-V3.2-style 671B MoE/MLA model on 16 NVIDIA H20 GPUs shows TP16 is KV-capacity-limited to ~70 concurrent 8K requests; sparse attention removes the KV-bandwidth term but not the capacity wall; EP16+DP-attention raises the capacity wall to ~644.

### CTA-Pipelining: A Latency-Oriented Spatial Scaling Method for Multi-GPU Systems

- **ArXiv:** [`2607.07862`](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07862v1) | **PDF:** [📄](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.07862v1)
- **作者:** Tingkai Liu, Muralidhar Andoorveedu, Sanjoy Das, Sanjay Patel, Volodymyr Kindratenko
- **标签:** `cs.DC, cs.LG`

**中文摘要：**

本文提出 CTA-Pipelining，一种面向多 GPU 共享内存系统的延迟导向空间扩展执行范式。与张量并行（TP）不同，CTA-Pipelining 在 CTA（协作线程数组）级别建立依赖关系，允许跨 GPU 并发执行有依赖的内核。在 8-GPU H200 和 B200 系统上使用 CUTLASS、cuBLAS 和 NCCL 库的实验表明，在 MLP 的 2 层 GEMM 上，CTA-Pipelining 相比 micro-batching 降低延迟 31.8%，相比 TP 降低 29.6%，且可与 TP 正交组合进一步优化延迟边界。

**English Abstract:**

We introduce CTA-pipelining, an execution paradigm designed to exploit shared-memory multi-GPU systems. As a latency-oriented spatial scaling technique, CTA-pipelining leverages dependencies at the Cooperative Thread Array level, enabling concurrent execution of dependent kernels across GPUs. Results on 8-GPU H200 and B200 systems show on 2-layer GEMM (MLP operation), CTA-pipelining reduces latency by up to 31.8% compared to micro-batching, and 29.6% compared to Tensor Parallelism (TP). It can also be combined with TP as an orthogonal scaling dimension.

### A Practical Investigation of Training-free Relaxed Speculative Decoding

- **ArXiv:** [`2607.08690`](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08690v1) | **PDF:** [📄](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.08690v1)
- **作者:** Guoxuan Xia, Luka Ribar, Paul Balanca
- **标签:** `cs.LG, cs.AI`

**中文摘要：**

本文系统研究了免训练宽松投机解码（Relaxed Speculative Decoding, RSD）技术的实际效果。RSD 通过放宽验证标准接受更多草稿令牌以换取加速，但引入采样偏差。实验发现：RSD 在代码生成和摘要等结构化任务上加速最显著，在知识密集型任务上增益有限，在创意写作上几乎无提升。重要发现：宽松方法需要仔细评估下游能力退化（与无损投机解码不同），且许多宽松方法依赖优质的草稿模型，不适合轻量级专用多令牌预测草稿器。

**English Abstract:**

This paper practically investigates training-free relaxed speculative decoding techniques. Standard speculative decoding is lossless. Recent work argues that relaxing this guarantee can yield further speed-ups. We unify existing approaches within a shared framework, benchmark them on contemporary settings, and distil takeaways for practitioners. Important takeaways include: relaxation requires considerable capability evaluation unlike lossless speculative decoding, and many relaxed approaches rely on a drafter that is a good language model, making them unsuited for lightweight dedicated multi-token-prediction drafters.

### GIFT: Geometry-Informed Low-precision Gradient Communication for LLM Pretraining

- **ArXiv:** [`2607.07494`](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07494v1) | **PDF:** [📄](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.07494v1)
- **作者:** Jieying Wang, Shuyuan Fan, Mingkai Zheng, Zhao Zhang
- **标签:** `cs.DC, cs.LG`

**中文摘要：**

本文提出 GIFT，一种几何感知的梯度通信方法，用于 LLM 预训练中的低位梯度压缩。现有方法在欧几里得空间量化梯度，由于高度各向异性梯度导致方向相关失真。GIFT 将梯度转换到近各向同性空间后再量化，使低位表示更忠实于高位对应物。在 64 节点 NVIDIA GH200 Superchip 上 Llama-600M 预训练实验中，GIFT 减少端到端预训练时间 7.6%，且相比直接 FP8 通信在下游任务保留方面表现更优。

**English Abstract:**

We present GIFT, a geometry-informed gradient scaling method that performs low-precision communication in geometry-aware coordinates. By transforming gradients into a near-isotropic space before quantization, GIFT makes low-precision representations substantially more faithful to their high-precision counterparts. Results show GIFT reduces the end-to-end pretraining time of Llama-600M by 7.6% on 64 NVIDIA GH200 Superchips, while improving downstream task preservation over direct Euclidean FP8 communication.

- **作者:** Truong-Thanh Le, Hoang-Loc La, Amir Taherkordi, Frank Eliassen, Phuong Hoai Ha, Peiyuan Guan
- **标签:** `cs.DC`

**中文摘要：**

本文研究了 NVIDIA GPU 在执行 ML/AI 工作负载时的频率缩放行为。初步发现：在较低性能 GPU 上，运行频率受近期工作负载历史强烈影响（通常在 80ms 窗口内）。这一行为挑战了现有最先进 ML 延迟预测技术的共同假设——即单个 GPU 内核延迟相互独立。GPU 的动态频率缩放引入内核间依赖，导致简单累加单内核测量值无法准确估计总执行时间。本文还提出了利用这一发现改进延迟预测模型、GPU 内核重排序策略以及 NAS 驱动的频率/延迟/能耗感知模型设计的若干方向。

**English Abstract:**

This work presents ongoing research on the frequency scaling behavior of NVIDIA GPUs when executing ML/AI workloads. Our preliminary findings show that on lower-performance GPUs, the operating frequency is strongly affected by the recent workload history, typically within an 80ms window. This behavior challenges a common assumption underlying several state-of-the-art ML latency-prediction techniques, which treat individual GPU kernel latencies as independent and therefore estimate total execution time by summing isolated per-kernel measurements. Our results indicate that such an assumption does not always hold.
