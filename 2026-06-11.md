### [2606.11806](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11806) External Experience Serving in Production LLM Systems: A Deployment-Oriented Study of Quality-Cost Trade-offs

- **作者**: Lin Sun, Heming Zhang, Xiangzheng Zhang
- **分类**: cs.CL

**English Abstract:**

Production LLM systems accumulate reusable operational experience, but the practical deployment issue is not merely whether such experience can help. It is how different serving strategies trade off quality against online cost under realistic constraints. Injecting external experience can improve task quality, yet it also increases prompt burden, latency, and serving pressure. We study external experience serving as a deployment-oriented quality-cost trade-off problem. We evaluate this trade-off across multiple serving strategies—including raw retrieval, offline post-training distillation, and online distillation—under realistic constraints such as budget, latency SLO, and context window limits. We find that offline distillation dominates when the budget is available, whereas raw retrieval is preferred under tight budget. Online distillation is never the preferred strategy under the constraints we consider. Our analysis sheds light on the load characteristics and deployment conditions that determine the optimal serving strategy, and provides actionable insights for practitioners deploying external experience in production LLM systems.

**中文摘要:**

生产级LLM系统积累了可复用的运行经验，但实际部署的问题不仅仅在于这些经验是否有帮助，更在于不同的服务策略如何在真实约束下权衡质量与在线成本。注入外部经验可以提高任务质量，但也会增加提示负担、延迟和服务压力。本文将外部经验服务作为一个面向部署的质量-成本权衡问题来研究，评估了多种服务策略——包括原始检索、离线后训练蒸馏和在线蒸馏——在预算、延迟SLO和上下文窗口限制等真实约束下的表现。研究发现，当预算充足时离线蒸馏占优，而在预算紧张时原始检索更优；在线蒸馏在考虑的约束条件下从未成为首选策略。

### [2606.12154](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12154) The Brain That Goes Quiet: Serving a Large Model's Knowledge at 131 Tokens per Second on an 8 GB Laptop by Removing the Large Model from the Runtime Path

- **作者**: Myeong Jun Jo
- **分类**: cs.PF

**English Abstract:**

In earlier work I showed that a 35B-class Mixture-of-Experts model can be loaded and executed on a consumer laptop with 8 GB of GPU memory. That result solved a placement problem and immediately exposed a different one: even correctly placed, the large model needed roughly four seconds to answer, because it was still being invoked at every query. This paper documents what happened when I stopped invoking it. During an offline phase, the large model reads source documents and writes verified answers into a local knowledge base. During online serving, the large model is removed from the runtime path entirely: it is not loaded, not warmed up, and not invoked. Queries are answered directly from the local knowledge base at 131 tokens per second, consuming roughly 12 watts. The client-server architecture serves multiple concurrent sessions. This paper reports the throughput and accuracy of this design on three personal knowledge-base tasks: Q&A over a single book, over multiple books, and over several years of personal journal entries.

**中文摘要:**

在之前的工作中，作者展示了35B级MoE模型可以在8GB GPU内存的消费级笔记本上加载和执行。但即便正确放置，大模型每次查询仍需约四秒。本文记录了将大模型从运行时路径中移除后的结果：在离线阶段，大模型读取源文档并将验证后的答案写入本地知识库；在线服务时，大模型完全不加载、不预热、不调用。查询直接从本地知识库以每秒131 token的速度回答，功耗约12瓦。该客户端-服务器架构支持多并发会话。本文报告了该设计在三个个人知识库任务上的吞吐量和准确率。

### [2606.12400](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12400) Doc-to-Atom: Learning to Compile and Compose Memory Atoms

- **作者**: Xingjian Diao, Wenbo Li, Yashas Malur Saidutta, Avinash Amballa, Lazar Valkov
- **分类**: cs.CL, cs.IR

**English Abstract:**

Long input sequences are central to document understanding and multi-step reasoning in Large Language Models, yet the quadratic cost of attention makes inference both memory-intensive and slow. Context distillation mitigates this by compressing contextual information into model parameters, and recent work such as Doc-to-LoRA amortizes context distillation into a single forward pass that generates one LoRA adapter per document. However, producing a single monolithic adapter for all queries leads to suboptimal memory usage and increasing overhead for long-form multi-document tasks, and the flat representation fails to capture hierarchical structure. In this work, we introduce Doc-to-Atom, a general framework that learns to decompose document context into a set of modular memory atoms. Each atom captures a self-contained piece of domain knowledge, represented as a small LoRA adapter or soft prompt. During inference, Doc-to-Atom dynamically composes only relevant atoms based on the input query, achieving efficient context utilization. We evaluate Doc-to-Atom on long-document QA and multi-document reasoning benchmarks. Results show that Doc-to-Atom achieves comparable accuracy to full-context baselines while reducing the per-query prefill cost by up to 7x and memory by 5x. Compared to monolithic Doc-to-LoRA, Doc-to-Atom reduces memory by up to 3x on long multi-document tasks while improving accuracy by 2.3%.

**中文摘要:**

长输入序列对LLM的文档理解和多步推理至关重要，但注意力的二次成本使推理既内存密集又缓慢。上下文蒸馏通过将上下文信息压缩到模型参数中来缓解这一问题。本文提出Doc-to-Atom，一个将文档上下文分解为一组模块化记忆原子的通用框架。每个原子捕获一个完整的领域知识片段，表示为小型LoRA适配器或软提示。推理时，Doc-to-Atom根据输入查询动态组合仅相关的原子，实现高效的上下文利用。在长文档QA和多文档推理基准上，Doc-to-Atom达到与全上下文基线相当的准确率，同时将每次查询的prefill成本降低7倍、内存降低5倍。

### [2606.12370](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12370) Breaking Entropy Bounds: Accelerating RL Training via MTP with Rejection Sampling

- **作者**: Yucheng Li, Huiqiang Jiang, Yang Xu, Jianxin Yang, Yi Zhang
- **分类**: cs.LG, cs.CL

**English Abstract:**

Reinforcement learning (RL) has become a key component in modern large language models, yet the rollout stage remains the key bottleneck in RL training pipelines. Although Multi-Token Prediction (MTP) offers a natural solution to accelerate rollouts through speculative decoding, many studies have observed that MTP acceptance rates degrade significantly during RL training, leading to limited speedup performance. To address this bottleneck, we present Bebop, a systematic study of MTP in LLM post-training. Through extensive analysis of MTP behavior across various RL training stages, models, and datasets, we identify that the fundamental limitation is tied to an entropy gap between the draft and target distributions, which constrains the maximum achievable acceptance rate during RL. Critically, this entropy gap is inherent to the MTP training objective, making the acceptance rate degradations unavoidable under standard MTP. Motivated by this analysis, we propose Bebop-rs, which incorporates rejection sampling on MTP's multi-token predictions to break the entropy bound and substantially improve acceptance rates. Across multiple training runs and model scales, Bebop-rs achieves 28−42% end-to-end RL training speedup compared to standard MTP, and up to 1.8−2.3x speedup over autoregressive baselines.

**中文摘要:**

强化学习（RL）已成为现代大语言模型的关键组成部分，但rollout阶段仍然是RL训练流程的主要瓶颈。虽然多token预测（MTP）通过投机解码提供了加速rollout的自然方案，但许多研究观察到MTP在接受率在RL训练期间显著下降。本文提出Bebop，对LLM后训练中MTP的系统性研究。研究发现根本限制在于草案分布和目标分布之间的熵差距，这限制了RL期间可达到的最大接受率。基于此分析，提出Bebop-rs，在MTP的多token预测上引入拒绝采样以突破熵界，显著提高接受率。在多个训练运行和模型规模上，Bebop-rs相比标准MTP实现了28-42%的端到端RL训练加速，相比自回归基线最高达1.8-2.3倍加速。

### [2606.12243](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12243) VIA-SD: Verification via Intra-Model Routing for Speculative Decoding

- **作者**: Yuchen Xian, Yang He, Yunqiu Xu, Yi Yang
- **分类**: cs.CL, cs.AI

**English Abstract:**

Speculative decoding (SD) addresses the high inference costs of LLMs by having lightweight drafters generate candidates for large verifiers to validate in parallel. Existing draft-verify methods use binary decisions: accept or fully recompute. Yet we find that many rejected tokens can be verified correctly by a slim submodel derived from the full verifier via intra-model routing, instead of the full verifier. This motivates our slim-verifier to handle tokens requiring moderate verification resources, reserving the full verifier for the most challenging tokens. We propose VIA-SD, which trains a lightweight routing module that assigns each candidate token to either the slim-verifier or the full-verifier based on its verification difficulty, achieving a finer-grained accept-recompute spectrum. On MT-Bench and Spec-Bench, VIA-SD achieves up to 2.5x speedup over vanilla SD and integrates seamlessly with existing SD methods by only modifying the verification stage.

**中文摘要:**

投机解码（SD）通过让轻量级草稿模型生成候选token，让大型验证器并行验证，来解决LLM的高推理成本问题。现有的验证方法使用二元决策：接受或完全重新计算。然而本文发现，许多被拒绝的token可以通过从完整验证器中通过模型内路由提取的瘦子验证器正确验证。本文提出VIA-SD，训练一个轻量级路由模块，根据验证难度将每个候选token分配到瘦验证器或完整验证器，实现了更细粒度的接受-重计算频谱。在MT-Bench和Spec-Bench上，VIA-SD相比原始SD实现了最高2.5倍加速，且仅需修改验证阶段即可与现有SD方法无缝集成。

**English Abstract:**

Large language models (LLMs) are widely used to tackle complex tasks with autonomous workflows. Recently, reusable natural language skills have emerged as a popular paradigm to inject procedural knowledge into LLM applications. Since popular skills are often invoked repeatedly, placing their full text in every context significantly increases prefill cost and latency. While text compression techniques have the potential to solve this problem, most existing methods are designed to compress factual knowledge and struggle to capture the hierarchical information and detailed procedural steps in skills. ### [2606.11718](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11718) Making Locality-aware GEMM Compatible with Page-Granularity Placement on Chiplet GPUs

- **作者**: Euijun Chung, Jae Hyung Ju, Hyesoon Kim
- **分类**: cs.AR

**English Abstract:**

Multi-chiplet GPUs scale compute throughput and high-bandwidth memory (HBM) capacity, but their non-uniform memory system makes locality between chiplets and their data critical to the GPU's performance and energy efficiency. Locality-aware scheduling and data placement identify which data should reside near each chiplet. However, in general matrix multiplication (GEMM), locality-aware data placement often becomes incompatible with a fixed page-granularity data interleaving, since the optimal granularity for GEMM (sub-block level) is finer than the page granularity enforced by the OS. We propose a general approach, Adaptive Page Interleaving (API), that carries out GEMM with page-level compatibility by selecting suitable data layouts at software-level with the awareness of the interleaving structure. API enables locality-aware GEMM with minimal chiplet idling and near-ideal computation-communication overlap, even under OS-imposed page interleaving. We evaluate API across diverse GEMM workloads, including those in LLM inference and training, and demonstrate that API outperforms the best prior locality-aware GEMM on chiplet GPUs by up to 3.33x and achieves near-ideal strong scaling.

**中文摘要:**

多芯粒GPU扩展了计算吞吐量和高带宽内存（HBM）容量，但其非均匀内存系统使得芯粒与其数据之间的局部性对GPU性能和能效至关重要。然而在通用矩阵乘法（GEMM）中，局部性感知的数据放置往往与固定的页面粒度数据交错不兼容，因为GEMM的最优粒度（子块级别）比OS强制执行的页面粒度更细。本文提出自适应页面交错（API），通过软件级别选择合适的数据布局，在页面级兼容性下执行GEMM。API使局部性感知的GEMM即使在OS施加的页面交错下也能实现最小芯粒空闲和近乎理想的计算-通信重叠。在包括LLM推理和训练在内的多样化GEMM工作负载上，API相比芯片GPU上先前的局部性感知GEMM最高提升3.33倍，并实现了近乎理想的强扩展。

### [2606.11716](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11716) A Fast Locality Simulator for GEMM Design-Space Exploration on Multi-Chiplet GPUs

- **作者**: Euijun Chung, Jae Hyung Ju, Hyesoon Kim
- **分类**: cs.AR

**English Abstract:**

Multi-chiplet GPUs split memory into local and remote HBM regions across a silicon interposer, and reducing the remote HBM traffic is crucial for the performance and energy efficiency of multi-chiplet GPUs. For general matrix multiplication (GEMM), the dominant operator in large language models (LLMs), the resulting inter-chiplet traffic depends strongly on kernel choices such as operand layout, CUDA core configuration, and tile sizes. However, exploring the joint design space of GEMM kernel parameters and chiplet data placement is difficult because each configuration must be evaluated through full-system simulation or real hardware execution. We use Fastsocket to explore over 4,000 configurations and show that optimal choices vary substantially across GEMM shapes. Fastsocket's predictions closely match real hardware measurements on a dual-chiplet GPU.

**中文摘要:**

多芯粒GPU将内存分割为硅中介层上的本地和远程HBM区域，减少远程HBM流量对多芯粒GPU的性能和能效至关重要。对于大语言模型中的主要算子GEMM，产生的芯粒间流量强烈依赖于操作数布局、CUDA核心配置和tile大小等内核选择。由于每个配置需要通过全系统模拟或真实硬件执行来评估，探索GEMM内核参数和芯粒数据放置的联合设计空间非常困难。本文提出Fastsocket，一个用于GEMM的快速局部性模拟器，在10秒内估算单个配置的远程流量，以前所未有的速度实现对多芯粒GPU上GEMM的设计空间探索。Fastsocket模拟了GEMM的分层计算与芯粒内存系统之间的相互作用，并支持跨操作数布局、分块策略和交错策略的敏感性分析。

### [2606.12280](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12280) Holding the FP8 Quality Ceiling at 8-Bit Weights and Activations: INT8 and GGUF Post-Training Quantization of Ideogram 4.0 for Consumer GPUs

- **作者**: *未公开完整作者列表*
- **分类**: cs.LG

**English Abstract:**

Post-training quantization lets large text-to-image diffusion transformers run on consumer GPUs, yet the hardware-specific trade-offs are seldom measured directly. We quantize Ideogram 4.0 — a 9.3B flow-matching diffusion transformer (DiT), shipped as two separate-weight copies of a single-stream 34-layer backbone for classifier-free guidance and conditioned by a Qwen3-VL-8B encoder — for Ampere RTX 4090 (compute 8.9, INT8 tensor core) and RDNA3 RX 7900XTX (FP8 stream processor, no INT8 hardware). - **作者**: Chitral Patil

Existing load-balancing systems target pre-training by relying on historical step-level statistics. However, these methods fail under the unique workload dynamics of RL post-training: the step-level load is stable, but the tiny batch sizes processed during micro-steps cause severe, high-frequency load fluctuations. ### [2606.12343](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12343) Fair Comparison of Scheduling Algorithms on Heterogeneous Edge Clusters: A Continuous Adaptive Benchmark

- **作者**: *未公开完整作者列表*
- **分类**: cs.DC

**English Abstract:**

Modern Artificial Intelligence (AI) workloads deployed across the heterogeneous tiers of an edge-cloud continuum must satisfy multi-dimensional Service Level Objectives (SLOs) over latency, throughput, and output quality. For each incoming task, the scheduler picks both a target node and a processing mode (e.g., full or reduced inference precision). We call this class of problems Continuous Adaptive Scheduling (CAS).
